UK updates

A stabbing incident has been reported in Southport in Northwest of
England where multiple people are feared dead. Police said that a knife
has been seized and a man has been detained.

According to a report in BBC, there are a “number of reported casualties”. Confirming the deaths, Merseyside Police said there are a
number of reported casualties and added that more details will be
confirmed. The Merseyside Police, in a statement, said, "We can confirm
that emergency services are in Southport following a major incident this
morning, Monday 29 July."
